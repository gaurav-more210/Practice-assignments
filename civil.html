Vision:

To be the department of international standards that focuses on the development of entrepreneurs and domain specific professionals on the platform of technology enabled learning systems and industrial interface..

Mission:

  • To provide technical education.
  • To prepare competitive students for employment/self-employment.
  • To focus on developing the professional skills as well as the values

Program Educational Objectives

  • To ensure that graduates will have a mastery of fundamental knowledge, problem solving skills, engineering experimental abilities, and design capabilities necessary for entering civil engineering career and/or graduate school.
  • To incorporate verbal and written communication skills necessary for successful professional practice.
  • Demonstrate knowledge of management principles and engineering techniques for effective project management.
  • To prepare graduates to deal with ethical and professional issues, taking into account the broader societal implications of civil engineering.

Program Specific Outcomes

  • Graduates will able to apply fundamental knowledge, problem solving skills, engineering experimental abilities, and design capabilities necessary for entering civil engineering career.
  • Graduates will be able to demonstrate knowledge of engineering techniques for effective project management and professional development to face emerging challenges.
  • Graduates will be able to apply their technical and professional skills to be nationally competitive for employment/self employment.
